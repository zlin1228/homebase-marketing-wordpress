Icons

We have access to all of Font Awesome's icons out of the box: https://fortawesome.github.io/Font-Awesome/icons/

Additionally, we have created our own custom font with https://icomoon.io/#docs. SVG FTW.

"That's great but I need a blue timeclock..."

Fear not, friend! Style icons with color and font-size as you please.

Form Elements

Some notes on our forms:

  • Labels should exist outside of the input. When labels are used as placeholders, users can forget what/why they're typing as they type over the label itself.
  • We use the help of a plugin to get flat checkbox/radio inputs: https://github.com/flatlogic/awesome-bootstrap-checkbox
