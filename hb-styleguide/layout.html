Spacing Helpers

Introducing a new way of adding whitespace...

Syntax: <padding|margin><location><size>

Use these to modify the space between elements.

Types

  • p - Padding
  • m - Margin

Locations

  • a - All
  • t - Top
  • r - Right
  • b - Bottom
  • l - Left
  • h - Horizontal
  • v - Vertical

Sizes

  • n - none (0px)
  • xxxs - xxx-small (3px)
  • xxs - xx-small (7px)
  • xs - x-small (10px)
  • s - small (15px)
  • m - medium (20px)
  • l - large (25px)
  • xl - x-large (30px)
  • xxl - xx-large (40px)
  • xxxl - x-large (60px)

Examples

<!-- Add medium size padding to all sides --!>
<div class="pam"></div>

<!-- Add large size margin to right side --!>
<div class="mrl"></div>

<!-- Add x-small padding to top and bottom (vertical) --!>
<div class="pvxs"></div>

<!-- Remove left and right margins (horizontal) --!>
<div class="hmn"></div>
